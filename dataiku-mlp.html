Summary

Sometime in January 2023, in my quest for self development, I discovered Dataiku software, an artificial intelligence and machine learning software owned by Dataiku Co.. Dataiku Academy is the company's dedicated learning and development platform designed to develop the software users from entry level to experts and specialists. The platform is entirely free as they're a relatively new and fast growing corporation that needs more and more clients to understand and use the software, but also experts and specialists.

Certification at Dataiku is subject to completion of course, hands-on labs and a projects that consists of real data transformation(ETL/ELT) and a multiple choice exam based on the course and dedicated project(s).

Companies that use Dataiku software include General Electric, BNP Paribas, Zurich, CISCO, Uniliver, Deloitte, UBSOFT, just to name a few.

When I decided that this was an excellent tool to add to my intellectual arsenal, I dedicated the time I had to learning the software; this effort ultimately helped in developing knowledge, experience, and in earning me the reckognitions details herein.
