Summary

First introduced to Azure when I worked as Brand Ambassador for Microsoft back in 2017, during one of the ambassador's development Seminars at Microsoft headquarter in Reading, UK. Worked with Azure at NHS Digital on some level; used the platform to host the website for Happy Fruit co., a self owned affiliate marketing company run for a little over a year in the midst of the pandemic to help get by, and as a measure to circumvent confinements restrictions, at which point I had developed a desire to gain expertise in cloud computing in general, and in particular the Azure platform. Due to some unfortunate circumstances beyond my control, Happy Fruit co had to be closed.

However, to help with Azure development, kept the Azure account created for my former company; this helped get access to Azure resources at will, which in turn ultimately was crucial for labs and exams preparations over the years. Fast forward to five(5) years later, effort paid off! on 15th August 2022 I completed all requirements to become the first person to pass Microsoft Azure's "AZ-305: Designing Microsoft Azure Infrastructure Solutions" test at Taitec Manchester test center, to certify as Microsoft Azure Solutions Architect Expert.

As of today, earned a total of 246 badges and 40 trophies. (Microsoft issues trophies and badges to denote achieving new milestones. I renewed my certificate only 6 months after being awarded with the said certificate and I keep up with changes on the platform as much as I do with personal development in general.)
