Personal Story

Originating from the Republic of Congo, I obtained a Physics degree before moving to England in year 2000. Despite initial financial challenges leading to discontinuation of my BSc Computing at Leeds University, I soon secured a comprehensive computing qualification through an intensive Microsoft/City & Guild partnership program in PC Engineering and Network Technology.

Balancing a passion for show business and IT, I was predominantly self-employed until 2018 when I committed fully to IT. This marked the beginning of an ongoing journey of self-development, where I pursued further education and business development courses in computing.

Initially drawn to CISCO for my networking background, my focus rapidly shifted towards cloud computing, anticipating its future significance. I delved into Azure, combining self-employment to facilitate my qualifications. Despite lacking solid programming experience, I adapted on-the-go to complete tasks for my Azure journey, learning a programming language at a time among other skills as and when needed.

Over six years and counting, I progressed from fundamentals to becoming a certified Microsoft Azure Solutions Architect Expert, expanding into DevOps, data engineering, and security.

Identifying the need for broader computer science recognition, I undertook research leading me to CS50 at Harvard University. Unlearning to learn helped eliminate poor habits developped in the past and create room for more and better, facilitating a much needed refurbishment; the program surpassed my expectations, providing not only the desired coding experience but also intellectual challenges crucial for my career.

Diligently working on every single assignments and beyond on average 10 hours daily, 7 days a week, completed CS50 in just under 4 month with maximum score on all but 1 unit. CS50 has not only honed my programming skills but has instilled in me the thinking process of a computer scientist.

Now seeking a challenging and dynamic position that aligns with extensive skills in IT operations, Azure administration, and data engineering. With a proven track record in successfully navigating complex projects, I am eager to contribute my expertise to a forward-thinking organization. I aspire to work collaboratively in an environment that encourages innovation, values professional growth, and offers opportunities to make a significant impact.

Open to varied roles that leverage my diverse skill set, I am excited about the prospect of contributing to the success of a cutting-edge team.
