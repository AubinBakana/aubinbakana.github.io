Summary

Following many weeks of dedication, focus, discipline, despite the daily frustration, working virtually 7 days a week for an average of 10 hours daily for just over 3 months, on 2nd Nov 2023 I successfully completed CS50 program at Harvard University! After earning maximum score on virtually all 9 labs and problem sets (i.e., programming assignments) and final project, finally awarded certificate of completion for CS50, an online version of Harvard university's largest computer program. Deadline for the course being 31/12/23, completion was a few weeks ahead of schedule.

To complete the program, in parrallel, took additional courses namely CS50W, CS50 Python, advanced Git/GitHub, and more. Languages learned throughout the program include C, Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, Flask & more.

Furthermore, as part of an AI deep dive effort, took time to complete related CS50P course work projects and assignments; thanks to previous knowledge and full time dedication, this was completed within 4 weeks. Thus, on 3rd June 2024, earned CS50 Python certificate with 99.70% mark.

CS50W: Web Programming with Python & JavaScript.

Transforming Learning into Impactful Technology and Business Success

I began the CS50W course in October 2024. As of today, May 8th, 2025, I have successfully completed all five required assignments, although I only submitted 3 which all scored 100%. However, due to limited computer resources(struggling with a modest 64GB Pentium laptop), and the founding of my startup, Baobab Logix LTD, I purposefully delayed completing the program. That's because I realized that securing funding for my company would not only drive its growth but also provide the necessary equipment to complete my final project without being constrained by my device’s limitations. Now, I’m applying my knowledge to develop three commercial applications using Django, turning my learning into real-world solutions. One of these projects will serve as my final project, seamlessly merging my coursework with my entrepreneurial vision. This approach allows me to integrate learning with innovation, transforming education into tangible, impactful results.
Projects developed here include:

  1. - Google Search: A front-end design mimmicking Google Search, Google Image Search, and Google Advanced Search, focusing on form handling and query parameters.
  2. - Wiki: A Wikipedia-like platform where users can create, edit, and search for entries.
  3. - Commerce: An online auction site where users can list items, place bids, and leave comments.
  4. - Mail: A web-based email client allowing users to send, receive, and archive messages.
  5. - Network: A social media platform where users can follow others, post updates, and like posts.
  6. - Final Project: A self-directed web application integrating skills learned throughout the course.
An update on the project will be available by end of May 2025
